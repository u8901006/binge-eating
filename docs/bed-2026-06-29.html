Journal of pediatric and adolescent gynecology · GLP-1 Receptor Agonists in Adolescents: Emerging Endocrine, Reproductive, and Psychosocial Concerns.

#2 🎭 中實用性

Eating behaviors · The moderating role of valence-specific urgency on the association between positive and negative emotion regulation difficulties and disordered eating in a clinically elevated sample of college students.

研究顛覆傳統認知，發現當衝動性較低時，情緒調節困難與暴食等飲食障礙行為的關聯反而更強，強調情緒調節是獨立於衝動性的治療靶點。
